SOLO LEVELING

Synopsis

In a world where hunters with magical powers battle deadly monsters, Sung Jin-Woo starts as the weakest hunter but gains the ability to level up his powers dramatically. Follow his journey from being nicknamed "the World's Weakest" to becoming one of the most powerful hunters in existence.

Key Information

  • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure
  • Original Work: Chugong (Novel)
  • Studio: A-1 Pictures
  • Release Date: January 2024

Cast & Creative Team

  • Sung Jin-Woo: Aleks Le
  • Cha Hae-In: Rebekah Thomas
  • Director: Shunsuke Nakashige
  • Music: Hiroyuki Sawano
